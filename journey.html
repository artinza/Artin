Award

Klimaatdetectives — National Winner

Age 13 · Technasium · Gemeente Amstelveen · June 2022

As part of the Technasium to the Rescue programme — 1,000 students, 120 from Keizer Karel College — four groups presented climate adaptation solutions to the Municipality of Amstelveen and Wethouder Floor Gordon (Sustainability). Out of four finalists, Klimaatdetectives was named a winner.

The proposal: slope city streets to channel rainwater into deep basins, then convert the flow energy using a Tesla turbine — with potential conversion to hydrogen. Calculated projected savings of €2 million for the Netherlands. Artin was responsible for the 3D model, the solution design, and the core technical approach.

Age 13. Municipal government. €2M projected. Tesla turbine. That's the standard.

Work · Co-op

Brickeye — Engineering Co-op

February 2025 — June 2025 · In-Person · Toronto

Joined Brickeye — a construction risk mitigation company — as an engineering co-op. Delivered two complete internal tools from scratch over four months, both of which went into active use.

Tool 1 — Device Management Dashboard: Replaced manual sensor registration with a barcode-scan UI connecting Airtable to Monnit. Built live status monitoring, valve controls, and on own initiative — an Isolation Forest ML anomaly detection layer that flagged abnormal sensor behaviour from historical data. That last feature wasn't in the brief.

Tool 2 — Industrial Oven Controller: Remote control dashboard for a $50K+ CAD industrial oven via Raspberry Pi. CSV profile loading, custom profile builder, per-minute telemetry, RTD-based expected vs actual temperature analysis, and hard safety rate limiters to protect the equipment.

Two production tools. One co-op. The anomaly detection was never asked for — built because it was the right thing to add.

CONCEPT — 01
AI Tennis Ball Machine

A robot that retrieves the ball where you hit it and fires back from that exact angle — simulating real rally patterns. Coaches can program it to mimic Roger Federer's strategies, drill a specific shot repeatedly, or set custom spin, height, and speed. Combined with the smart wristband for full performance analysis.

CONCEPT — 02
Biometric Smart Home

A home that adapts to you in real time — not to your schedule, to your biology. A wristband tracks heart rate, stress, temperature, and oxygen. The house responds: dim the lights, play calming music, suggest a breathing exercise, adjust airflow. Extends the smart vent system into a full sensory-aware environment.
